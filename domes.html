Luxury Glamping in
Our Geodesic Domes

Explore the distinctive geodesic dome accommodations at Back Forty Glamping, where modern luxury meets the great outdoors. Each dome rental is crafted for comfort and privacy, set against the stunning backdrop of Meaford, Ontario. Find out more about how you can enjoy a unique, immersive nature experience without sacrificing comfort.

Forest Domes

Our forest domes are nestled among the tree line, elevated on wood decks, with private hot tubs. These domes overlook the wild forest (and you may even catch a glimpse of the pond below). The forest domes all have identical layouts and are all heated by propane fireplaces. These domes all have a transparent portion of the roof, as Back Forty Glamping is the perfect place to sleep under the stars. This stargazer window is larger in the winter and smaller in the summer to help regulate dome temperature depending on the season.

  • 2 Guests
  • 1 Bath
  • 1 Bed

Meadow Domes

Our Meadow Domes, positioned in open fields, offer expansive views of meadows and distant forests. Featuring private hot tubs, and resting on cement pads equipped with in-floor heating, they provide a warm, comforting atmosphere. Unlike our Forest Domes, these do not feature transparent ceilings; however, they have large front windows for stargazing directly from the comfort of your bed or while relaxing in the private hot tub, promising spectacular starry nights. (Note that the Capella meadow dome is specially designed for enhanced accessibility in both the kitchen and bathroom areas.)

  • 2 Guests
  • 1 Bath
  • 1 Bed
