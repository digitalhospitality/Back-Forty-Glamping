Luxury Glamping in
South Georgian Bay

Back Forty Glamping is a geodesic dome glamping resort in Ontario's South Georgian Bay. Our secluded domes, located on 26 spacious acres, feature comfortable bedding, relaxing hot tubs, a panoramic sauna and stunning views of the surrounding Ontario wilderness. The domes are available in all four seasons and include running water, electricity, kitchenettes and ensuite bathrooms. Experience privacy, comfort, and direct access to the area's best attractions.

When We Say
“Back Forty…”

We mean it. The term traditionally refers to the most remote, undeveloped part of a property, untouched and brimming with natural beauty. At Back Forty Glamping, that’s exactly what we offer. Here, you’re given a chance to experience nature intimately, while still enjoying the comforts and luxuries of home.

Different Domes; Different Experiences

At Back Forty Glamping, we offer two distinctive dome experiences: Forest Domes, for those who seek solitude among the trees, and Meadow Domes, which provide open vistas of fields and forests. Both options deliver luxury and comfort, each with unique features to enhance your stay in nature.

Forest Domes

Our Forest Domes, set along a tree line on elevated wooden decks, offer intimate views of the wilderness and a quiet pond. Each dome has a propane fireplace, stargazing roof, and private hot tub. The domes have identical layouts, but subtle differences in decor.

See Domes

Meadow Domes

Positioned in open fields, our Meadow Domes showcase views of the meadows and distant forest line. Each dome has a concrete base with in-floor heating and large windows for stargazing from your bed or hot tub, and the same luxurious amenities as the Forest Domes.

Plenty of Space

Our geodesic domes are 70-100 feet apart, each surrounded by privacy fencing, to guarantee a peaceful, relaxing, and private stay. This thoughtful layout ensures that guests enjoy their own secluded sanctuary, perfectly blending solitude with the beauty of the natural surroundings.
